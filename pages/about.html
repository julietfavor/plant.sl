Our Mission & Vision

Mission Statement

Plant Network Sierra Leone exists to advance the Great Commission by planting healthy churches, discipling believers, providing quality education, empowering communities economically, and delivering humanitarian relief—all rooted in biblical faith and sustainable practices.

Statement of Faith

We believe and affirm the following biblical truths:

The Trinity

We believe in one God eternally existing in three persons: God the Father, God the Son (Jesus Christ), and God the Holy Spirit. Each person of the Godhead is fully God and worthy of worship.

The Authority of Scripture

We believe the Bible—Old and New Testaments—is the inspired, authoritative, and trustworthy Word of God. It is our ultimate authority for faith and practice.

Salvation Through Christ

We believe that all humans are sinners and separated from God. Jesus Christ, God's Son, died on the cross to pay the penalty for our sins and rose from the dead on the third day. Salvation comes through faith in Jesus Christ alone.

The Great Commission

We believe Jesus commanded His followers to "go and make disciples of all nations." This is our primary calling and the foundation of our ministry.

The Kingdom of God

We believe the Kingdom of God is both present now and will be fully realized when Christ returns. We work to advance God's Kingdom through evangelism, discipleship, justice, and compassionate service.

Holistic Ministry

We believe the Gospel addresses the whole person—spiritual, physical, emotional, and economic. Therefore, we integrate spiritual discipleship with practical help and community development.

The Return of Christ

We believe Jesus Christ will return to judge all people and establish His eternal Kingdom. This hope motivates our urgency in proclaiming the Gospel.
