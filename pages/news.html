Featured Story

From Despair to Hope: Mama Fatima's Journey

📅 May 15, 2025 | 📍 Freetown

Mama Fatima is a 52-year-old widow who lost her husband five years ago. Left with four children and no income, she struggled daily to provide even one meal. When she heard about our savings group program, she took a chance.

"I was ashamed," Mama Fatima recalls. "I had nothing to contribute. But the group welcomed me and helped me start saving just 1,000 Leones a week. Within six months, I had enough for a small loan."

With her loan, Mama Fatima started a small palm oil business. Today, she earns enough to send all four children to school, feed her family, and contribute to her church. But more importantly, she found hope.

"Plant Network gave me more than money," she says with tears in her eyes. "They showed me that I matter. That God hasn't forgotten me. Now I know my children have a future."

Impact: Through our economic empowerment program, families like Mama Fatima's are breaking cycles of poverty and building sustainable livelihoods.
