Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I receive a response?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please WhatsApp us.

What is your time zone?

We are based in Sierra Leone (West Africa Time - WAT, UTC+0). Business hours are Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 5 PM WAT.

Is my donation secure?

Yes. We use secure payment methods and handle all personal and financial information with the highest security standards. Plant Network is a registered NGO with full transparency and accountability.

Can I volunteer remotely?

Yes! Many of our volunteer opportunities can be done from anywhere. We need writers, designers, social media managers, and more. Learn more about remote volunteering.

How do I know my donation is used effectively?

We provide annual transparency reports and welcome inquiries about our operations. We're committed to stewardship and accountability. View our legal information.
