💰 Support Us Through Donations

Your generosity directly impacts families and communities in Sierra Leone. Whether you give $10 or $1,000, every donation matters and saves lives.

What Your Donation Does

$25 - Provides school materials for one student for a month

$50 - Sponsors a student's monthly school fees

$100 - Supports a vocational training course for one youth

$250 - Provides seed and tools for a farming cooperative family

🤝 Volunteer With Us

Your time and skills are invaluable. Whether you're in Sierra Leone or around the world, there are many ways you can serve.

Volunteering Opportunities

🌍

On-Ground in Sierra Leone

Join our team for short-term (weeks) or long-term (months/years) service. Help with evangelism, education programs, agricultural projects, or medical outreach.

⏱️ Flexible duration • 🏥 Housing provided • 📞 Full support

💻

Remote Support

Help from home! We need writers, social media managers, graphic designers, and technical support. Use your skills to advance the mission.

🏠 Work from anywhere • ⏰ Your schedule • 🌐 Make global impact

🗣️

Speaking & Advocacy

Share about our mission in your church, school, or organization. Raise awareness and mobilize support for communities in Sierra Leone.

📢 Use your platform • 👥 Build awareness • 💪 Inspire others

How to Volunteer

  1. Fill out our volunteer application
  2. We'll discuss opportunities that match your skills and interests
  3. Complete any required background checks
  4. Receive orientation and get connected with your serving role
  5. Start making a difference!

🤲 Partner With Us

Organizations, churches, and businesses can collaborate with us to multiply impact in Sierra Leone.

Partnership Types

⛪

Church Partnership

Your church can partner with us to support specific projects like student scholarships, school construction, or agricultural initiatives. Receive regular updates and prayer opportunities.

🏢

Corporate Partnership

Businesses can sponsor programs, donate materials, or provide employee volunteer opportunities while making a meaningful social impact.

🤝

NGO Collaboration

Other nonprofits can collaborate on complementary programs, share expertise, and expand our collective reach in Sierra Leone.

🎓

University Partnership

Schools can send student teams for internships, research projects, or service learning opportunities while supporting our communities.

Why Partner With Us?

✅ Proven impact in communities

✅ Transparent financial reporting

✅ Regular progress updates

✅ Flexible partnership models
